Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Chambabot is designed to help you manage your business securely and confidentially.

Last updated: July 13, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Chambabot through WhatsApp, we collect:

  • Phone Number: To identify your account
  • Text Messages: The notes and records you send about your business
  • Voice Messages: We transcribe audio to process your requests
  • Usage Information: Number of messages sent to manage your plan

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Process and respond to your messages
  • Generate analytics and reports for your business
  • Manage your subscription and plan limits
  • Improve our service based on aggregated usage
Important: We never sell, share, or disclose your business information to third parties. Your business data is confidential.

3. Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption
  • History is maintained according to your plan:
    • Free Plan: 3 days
    • Essential Plan: 30 days
    • Professional Plan: 6 months
    • Premium Plan: 12 months
  • Voice messages are processed and deleted immediately after transcription

4. Third-Party Services

To provide you the best service, we use:

  • WhatsApp Business API: For communication
  • 11Labs: For voice transcription (they don't store your audio)
  • Mistral AI: For natural language processing (they don't train on your data)
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your stored data
  • Export your information in readable format
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

6. Data Deletion

To delete your account and all your data:

  1. Send a message to Chambabot saying "Delete my account"
  2. Confirm deletion by responding "CONFIRM"
  3. All your data will be permanently deleted within 24 hours

You can also request deletion by contacting us directly.

7. Policy Changes

If we update this policy, we'll notify you through WhatsApp at least 30 days in advance.
